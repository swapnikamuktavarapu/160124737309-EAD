About CBIT

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is one of the premier engineering colleges in Hyderabad, established in 1979. It is affiliated with Osmania University and is known for providing quality education in engineering and technology.

The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering.

CBIT has a well-developed campus with modern infrastructure, including laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and hostels, creating a supportive environment for students.

The college focuses on academic excellence, innovation, and overall student development, preparing students for successful careers in their respective fields.
